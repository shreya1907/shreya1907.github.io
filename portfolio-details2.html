Analysis of lungs using CT scans by application of computational Image Processing

  • Software: MATLAB | Image Processing
  • Project date:18 Sept, 2018
  • Algorithm: RESNET-50
  • Dataset: Computed Tomography (CT) scan images
    from Tongji Hospital research team
  • Accuracy: 89.26%

What is the purpose of our Project?

• The purpose of this project is the analysis of lungs using Computed Tomography scans by application of computational image processing in MATLAB.
• This project proposes an adaptable COVID-19 identification technique during the pandemic, We have performed a simple convolution neural network (CNN) based model by utilizing lung CT images. And ultimately, ResNet-50 CNN architecture is employed for this design.
• For training and testing:
→ 349 COVID-19 lung CT frames
→ 397 NON COVID-19 CT frames
After completion, Accuracy of 89.2617% and Precision of 85% are obtained.

Getting to the Details

IMAGE PROCESSING and MACHINE LEARNING will be the dominant tool used with MATLAB’s SIMULINK.
The project uses image processing to increase the contrast of the CT-SCAN images of lungs and the CNN to detect and classify the COVID-19 pulmonary CT-SCAN images.
To train the software, a dataset consisting of various images of Computed Tomography (CT) scan samples of lungs is utilized. This dataset suffices the current need for training the image processing program. Detecting health issues through CT scan is one of the accessible and alternative methods in the health area and the accuracy of this method will increase using stable and accurate equipment in imaging.
