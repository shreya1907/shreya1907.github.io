MANDALA ILLUSTRATIONS

Something about Mandala...

• Mandalas often have radial harmony.

• The term 'mandala' arises in the Rigveda as the representation of the sections of the work, and Vedic rituals use mandalas such as the Navagraha mandala to this day.

• A mandala is a geometric cadre of symbols.

• A mandala generally represents the spiritual journey, starting from outside to the inner core, through layers.

• My personal preference for designing mandalas is by adding abstracts to the geometrical and symmetric designs.
